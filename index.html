Sea freight transit time depends on your destination: Asia 7-15 days, Middle East 20-30 days, Africa 20-45 days, South America 30-45 days, Europe & North America 25-40 days. I'll confirm the exact time in your quotation.
What warranty do you offer?
12-month warranty or 1,500-2,000 working hours, whichever comes first. Exact terms vary by model and are confirmed in the quotation.
What payment methods do you accept?
T/T (wire transfer): 30% deposit in advance, 70% balance before shipment. Minimum order is 1 unit. Bulk orders get discounted pricing.

All backhoe loaders can be customized with your preferred color at no extra cost. Over 40 attachments available. Customization lead time is 35-40 days.
