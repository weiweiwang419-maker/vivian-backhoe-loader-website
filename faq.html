Frequently Asked Questions

Everything buyers ask me about delivery, warranty, payment, customization, and quality. Can't find your answer? Just message me directly.

Delivery & Shipping
How long does delivery take to my country?+

Sea freight transit time depends on your destination:

  • Asia: 7–15 days
  • Middle East: 20–30 days
  • Africa: 20–45 days
  • South America: 30–45 days
  • Europe: 30–40 days
  • North America: 18–38 days
  • Oceania: 18–28 days
The exact time may vary depending on the shipping line, vessel schedule, weather, and customs clearance. I'll provide the estimated delivery time when preparing your quotation.

Which ports do you ship to?+

We ship to over 95% of ports worldwide. No matter where you are, we can find a route to get your machine there. Tell me your nearest port and I'll check the shipping options for you.

Do you handle customs clearance?+

Under FOB/CIF terms, we do not handle destination port customs clearance. However, we can recommend reliable local customs agents in your country to help streamline the process.

Warranty & After-Sales
What warranty do you offer?+

The complete machine comes with a 12-month warranty or 1,500–2,000 working hours, whichever comes first. The exact warranty period varies by model — I'll confirm the specific terms for your chosen machine in the quotation.

How do I get spare parts?+

We maintain stock of common spare parts and can ship them separately. Each machine also comes with a basic spare parts kit. For specific parts, just send me a photo or part number on WhatsApp and I'll arrange shipment.

Do you provide technical support after purchase?+

Yes. We provide remote technical support via video calls, detailed technical documentation, and ongoing assistance whenever you need it. Just reach out on WhatsApp or email.

Payment
What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept T/T (wire transfer): 30% deposit in advance, 70% balance before shipment.

What is the minimum order quantity?+

Minimum order is 1 unit. For bulk pricing: mini models 8+ units, mid-size 6+ units, large 3+ units. Bulk orders receive discounted pricing — contact me for a custom quote.

Customization
Can the machine be customized?+

All backhoe loaders can be customized with your preferred color at no extra cost. Customization lead time is 35–40 days depending on complexity.

What attachments are available?+

Over 40 attachments are available, including various bucket sizes, hydraulic breakers, crushers, snow brushes, fork frames, and more. Tell me what you need to do and I'll recommend the right attachment.

Can I choose the engine brand?+

Yes. Available engine brands include Yanmar, Cummins, Yuchai, Xinchai, and Yunnei. Different brands have different price points — I'll help you choose the best option for your budget and market.

Operation & Training
Do you provide operation training?+

Yes. We create customized training videos based on your specific needs. For qualified bulk dealers, we also provide on-site training.

Is the machine easy to operate for beginners?+

The machine is straightforward and easy to learn. Before operation, always check your surroundings and ensure the bucket and excavator arm are in a safe, raised position. We provide an operation manual and training videos with every machine.

Quality & Certification
What certifications does the machine have?+

All machines are CE and ISO9001 certified. We can also assist qualified dealers with local certification requirements in your country.

How do you ensure quality before shipping?+

Every machine undergoes strict testing before leaving the factory. We provide inspection videos for your approval and welcome you to visit the factory for in-person inspection at any time.

Can I visit your factory?+

You're welcome to visit our factory at any time. Airport pickup service can be arranged — just let me know your travel plans.
